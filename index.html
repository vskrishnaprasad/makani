About Makani

"Food is a universal language that speaks to the soul, transcending borders and cultures."

At Makani, we bring this philosophy to life through a harmonious blend of Indian and Arabic cuisine. Located in the heart of Edinburgh, our restaurant celebrates flavors and cultures, offering an unparalleled dining experience.
we believe food is more than just sustenance; it's an experience. Our menu combines rich Indian spices and aromatic Arabic herbs, bringing you the best of both worlds. From savory kebabs and biryanis to delightful hummus and manthi, every dish is prepared with the freshest ingredients and a passion for perfection.

We present authentic Arabian and Indian cuisine influenced by the South Indian palette for spicy and aromatic dishes and traditional cooking techniques. We are passionate about food and our chefs put their heart and soul into every dish that leaves our kitchen to provide the most authentic experience of South Indian and Arabic style cuisine. We will take you on a journey to explore the culinary of Middle East and Authentic taste of South India. Food plays a pivotal role in South Asian culture and dining out is often a grand event, a celebration. We take inspiration from our ancestors and we will ensure our customers to experience the diverse dishes.

Malabar Dum Biriyani

Chicken Dum Biriyan, Beef Dum Biriyani, Royal Dum Biriyani

Dum biryani is a slow cooked layered unique jeerakasala rice and blend of meat on bone with exotic spices and special malabar masalas. Served with Raita and pickles.Royal bhiriyani comes with extra whole pieces of Deep fried Quail, boiled egg and pieces of chicken 65 and popadom.

Mandi

Arabic mandi is a traditional dish that originated from Yemen. consisting mainly of meat and rice with a special blend of spices, cooked in a pit

Paal Porotta

Kerala Porotta layered with coconut milk,succulent pieces of beef or chicken, curry leaves, onions and fried egg.

Lamb Chops

Savor the smoky flavor of our marinated lamb chops, spiced to perfection and grilled over charcoal for a tender, juicy bite. A true delight for bold flavor lovers!

Tandoori Mix Grill

Tandoori chicken, chicken tikka, haryali tikka, lamb tikka, seekh kebab and lamb chops served with pilau rice/naan, mint sauce.
